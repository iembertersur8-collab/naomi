About Me

Expertise & Experience

Hello, I'm Tersur Iember Naomi.

I am a dedicated Book Formatting Specialist, Layout Designer, and Self-Publishing Consultant. My mission is to bridge the gap between a great manuscript and a visually stunning, publication-ready book.

I pride myself on a meticulous workflow, utilizing industry-standard tools to deliver pixel-perfect layouts, flawless EPUBs, and KDP-compliant files that pass Amazon's strict guidelines on the very first try.
